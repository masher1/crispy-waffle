About
Crispy-Waffle

Crispy Waffle is a company that was founded on the idea of having recipes every available for everybody at their fingertips. Crispy Waffle was built for people who have a passion for cooking and learning how to prepare food. From young children to the elderly, Crispy Waffle provides comfortability and easier access to all. Crispy Waffle was founded by Mrinal Mathur and Malkiel Asher, two Computer Engineering undergraduate students in CSE 389 and hail from the proud Orange Syracuse family. Crispy Waffle strives to make an impact in the food industry by providing accessibility to the general public to recipes that even professional chefs may use in industry!
